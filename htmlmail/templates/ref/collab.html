Mobile Development with Git, Gerrit & Jenkins

If you are doing mobile software development, this webinar is for you!

Are you building mobile enterprise apps? Learn how to integrate mobile app development into your software lifecycle – from planning to development and deployment. Focusing on Android apps, we’ll provide practical examples implementing an Agile delivery pipeline. This technically oriented webinar will benefit engineers and their managers responsible for the mobile app lifecycle.  iOS and Windows users should find this webinar useful.

What we will cover:

  • Mobile’s impact on ALM (application lifecycle management)
  • Building a Continuous Delivery (CD) pipeline for mobile apps
  • Real-life examples including Git and Gerrit, Jenkins and TeamForge
